📖 Our Roots

From the Savanna to You

CurioCraft Kenya partners with rural artisan cooperatives to preserve traditional crafts while creating sustainable livelihoods. Every purchase directly supports a family.

Fair Trade Commitment

We pay artisans above market rate and reinvest in community programs — schools, clinics, and craft training centres across rural Kenya.
